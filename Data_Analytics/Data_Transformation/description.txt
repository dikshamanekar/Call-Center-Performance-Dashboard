Data Transformation involves converting raw data from multiple heterogeneous sources into a clean,
standardized and analysis-ready format before loading it into the data warehouse. Transformation
ensures that the data stored in the warehouse is consistent, accurate and aligned with business rules-
ultimately improving the quality of reporting and decision-making.

Data Transformation:-

1.Feature engineering

2.Normalization / standardization

3.Aggregation and filtering

4.Encoding categorical data
